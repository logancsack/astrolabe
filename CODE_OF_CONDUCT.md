# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal insults or threats.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban users for violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainer via GitHub profile contact channels.
Include links/screenshots where possible.

## Attribution

This policy is adapted from common open-source community standards.
